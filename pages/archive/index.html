Traverse Imagery

As Zoë drove, her cameras captured a continuous record of the terrain — both for navigation and science. The image archive is organized by deployment, then by field day, then by camera and instrument.

A few selected images appear in the expedition narratives and gallery. The complete archive — hundreds of thousands of images — is available for download and browsing below.

Panoramic Mosaics

At key waypoints, Zoë's cameras swept through overlapping positions to capture full panoramas — some covering a complete 360 degrees. These were computationally stitched into high-resolution mosaics that scientists use to study landscape structure, geology, and life distribution.

Each panorama below links to a zoomable full-resolution viewer. Raw tile sets and source images are available for download.
